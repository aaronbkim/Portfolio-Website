artist, creator

My name is Aaron Kim. I am an artist and student from Palo Alto, California, who enjoys painting and drawing in various media and styles, as well as experimenting with different techniques to explore creative diversity in my work.

As an artist, I have a special opportunity to communicate messages that can inspire and empower others. Through my work, I aim to promote critical yet imaginative thinking, originality, and expressive freedom.

This page contains a compilation of the work I've done so far. Enjoy!
